CHICKEN TINDER

The most common difficulty in a relationship is deciding on where to eat; Chicken Tinder solves this problem. Much like the popular dating app Tinder, my application helps the users decide where to eat. Create a session, share the code with your friend, and swipe until you both click "yes" on the same restaurant.

WE TRIPPIN'

Going on vacation is great, but going with a group of close friends is even better! Though with more attendees, come more suggestions on what to do. Instead of sharing ideas via text or social media where they can be lost or forgotten about, We Trippin' creates an environment where all users who are added to a trip can contibute comments or suggestions in detail via categories and sub-categories.

* Disclaimer: Alcohol consumption may result in straying off the itinerary you create.
