NIGHTLIFE FLYERS
Some of the events I worked on were brand new, others were already established and fairly successful, though having an eye-catching flyer can pull in an even larger crowd. I was given complete reign on revamping the imagery for older events, and in turn I became the go-to designer for several LGBT+ nightlife parties. Because of the success, I also had the opportunity to work on imagery for an event in Canada!

LOGO DESIGN
San Francisco has a booming nightlife, with new weekly/monthly parties being organized regularly. With no previous experience in graphic design, I was reached out to by other LGBT+ community members to help create visuals for these events. Additionally, I've created several logos for not only my own branding, but for organizations I know through the several communities I'm part of.
